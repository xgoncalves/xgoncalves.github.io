Editor's preface

Xavier completed a Bachelor's and Master's degree in Design and Multimedia from the University of Coimbra, and holds a Specialist Title in Computer Science from the Polytechnic Institute of Coimbra. He currently resides in Coimbra and works as a product designer at Linkare. The following pages present, in chronological order, the most notable works from each year.

2013

Linkare

pp. 6–9

Linkare TI

Designer & developer · Lisbon

Began working as a designer and developer at Linkare TI in Lisbon. Founded in 2004, Linkare has offices in Lisbon, Coimbra, and Brussels, with over 50 employees. The company specialises in IT solutions: software development, system modernisation, and technology consulting across public administration, telecommunications, and financial services.

Over more than 12 years at Linkare, Xavier has worked across various business sectors, technologies, and clients, both nationally and internationally.

MOV.AI Robotics Engine Platform

Current · Product design

UX/UI design for MOV.AI, a robotics engine platform for autonomous mobile robots. The work spans low-code development tools, deployment interfaces, and fleet management: helping operators configure missions, define operational scenes, and manage heterogeneous robot fleets without drowning in complexity.

Making industrial robotics accessible through interfaces that respect the complexity of the domain without passing it on to the user.
